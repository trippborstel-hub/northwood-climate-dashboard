## Transaction Background

NovaCare Health Partners was acquired from a mid-market healthcare-focused private equity fund that had held the company since 2014. Under prior ownership, the platform grew from 12 to 18 clinics organically across the Mid-Atlantic and Southeast regions. The selling sponsor had achieved its target return and was harvesting Fund II investments.

The management team, led by Dr. Sandra Reeves (CEO since 2016), elected to roll 20% of their proceeds into the new transaction, signaling confidence in the continued growth trajectory. Northwood was attracted to the platform's established clinic operating model, therapist development pipeline, and the fragmented nature of the outpatient rehabilitation market.
