# NovaCare Health Partners -- Key Personnel Profiles

**CONFIDENTIAL -- Northwood Capital**
**Last Updated:** January 31, 2026

---

## Executive Summary

NovaCare Health Partners leadership team comprises four direct reports to the Board, led by CEO Dr. Sandra Reeves. The management team has been stable since acquisition, with all three original executives (CEO, CFO, COO) continuing in their roles through 6+ years of Northwood ownership. Two additional VP-level hires (VP Clinical Operations, VP Business Development) were made post-close to support platform expansion. Management equity totals approximately 8.0% of fully diluted equity, with $19.2M in rollover co-investment from the executive team (now fully vested). Overall flight risk is LOW across the leadership group.

|-------|--------|

| **Tenure** | 9 years (joined 2017, pre-dates Northwood ownership) |
| **Prior Experience** | Regional Operations Director, US Physical Therapy (5 years, oversaw 20-clinic region); Operations Manager, NovaBay Pharmaceuticals (3 years) |

| **Compensation** | $325K base + 60% target bonus + equity |
| **Employment Agreement** | Expires September 2026; renewal discussions to commence Q1 2026 |
| **Flight Risk** | **LOW** |

**Assessment:** Torres is the operational backbone of NovaCare. She manages 310+ FTEs across 28 clinics through 4 Regional Directors, with a hands-on style that is valued by clinic-level staff. Her US Physical Therapy background gives her direct experience managing multi-site PT operations at scale. She built NovaCare's clinic operating playbook, led the integration of both PhysioFirst and RehabWorks acquisitions, and is the primary sponsor of the therapist retention program (turnover reduced from 22% to 18% under her leadership).

She has also been the operational champion for procurement centralization ($1.0M annual savings) and will lead the 2026 de novo clinic openings. Her knowledge of the clinic-level operations -- from therapist scheduling to supply chain logistics -- is deep and difficult to replicate.

**Development Areas:** Could delegate more to Regional Directors; her hands-on style creates bottlenecks when managing 28 locations. The expansion to 30+ clinics will require further development of the middle management layer. Northwood Operating Partner Lisa Brennan is coaching Torres on organizational scaling.
