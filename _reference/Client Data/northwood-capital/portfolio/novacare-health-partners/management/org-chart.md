## Key Organizational Notes

1. **Lean corporate structure.** NovaCare operates with a deliberately lean corporate overhead -- 75 FTEs (19% of total) in non-clinic roles, with the remaining 310 FTEs (81%) in clinic operations. This structure supports the investment thesis of G&A leverage as clinics are added. The 2026 de novo expansion (2 clinics, ~28 FTEs) will increase clinic headcount without proportional corporate growth.

2. **Four Regional Directors provide management leverage.** Each Regional Director oversees 4-10 clinics and reports to the COO. This structure was formalized post-RehabWorks acquisition to manage the expanded 28-clinic footprint. Regional Directors handle day-to-day clinic performance management, freeing the COO to focus on strategic initiatives (de novo, retention, integration).

3. **Centralized billing is a shared services center.** The 22-person billing team operates as a centralized function under the CFO, processing claims for all 28 clinics. This was a key post-acquisition initiative that reduced DSO from 52 to 39 days and generates $2.5M in annual savings.
