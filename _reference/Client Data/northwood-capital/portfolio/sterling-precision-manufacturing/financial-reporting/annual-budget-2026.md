## Key Assumptions

| Assumption | Value | Sensitivity |
|------------|-------|-------------|
| Defense revenue growth | +21% | +-5%: +/-$3.6M revenue, +/-$1.1M EBITDA |
| Commercial aerospace growth | +7% | +-3%: +/-$1.4M revenue, +/-$0.4M EBITDA |
| Machinist wage inflation | 5.0% | +-1%: +/-$0.8M EBITDA |
| CNC utilization improvement | +170 bps (to 89%) | Each 100bps: ~$0.5M EBITDA |
| Raw material cost (aluminum, titanium) | Flat to +3% | Pass-through on most contracts; ~$0.3M exposure on fixed-price |
| Second add-on acquisition | Not included in budget | Would be incremental to budget |

---

## Risks to Budget

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Defense program delay (F-35 or satellite) | Medium | $3-5M revenue slippage | Diversified across 4 programs; only one is a new ramp |
| Machinist hiring shortfall | Medium | Production throughput constraint | Apprenticeship pipeline (35 current); overtime capacity |
| Raw material price spike | Low-Medium | $0.5-1.0M margin compression | Most contracts have pass-through clauses; strategic inventory on hand |
| Automation Phase II delay | Low | $0.4M savings delay | Equipment ordered; vendor committed to Q2 delivery |
