# Sterling Precision Manufacturing — Monthly Financial Package

**Period:** January 2026
**Prepared by:** Janet Chen, CFO
**Date:** February 15, 2026

---

## Executive Summary

January performance was in line with expectations, reflecting the typical Q1 seasonal maintenance dip in the industrial segment. Revenue of $13.5M was slightly below monthly budget ($13.8M) driven by planned maintenance downtime at two industrial customers. Defense revenue remained steady, and margins held despite lower volume. No material surprises. The company enters February with a healthy backlog and expects to track back to budget in Q1 as the industrial dip resolves.
