## Organizational Notes

1. **MicroTech Integration:** MicroTech Tooling operates as a semi-integrated division under the COO. Back-office functions (finance, HR, IT) were consolidated into Sterling corporate within 12 months of acquisition. MicroTech retains its own production management and customer relationships but reports through the VP of Manufacturing.

2. **VP of Engineering Hire:** The November 2025 addition of a VP of Engineering (former Boeing) is a strategic hire. This role was created to manage the growing complexity of defense program engineering — qualification testing, CMMC compliance, and coordination with defense prime engineering teams. The role did not exist previously; engineering had reported directly to the COO.

3. **Independent Director Search:** Northwood is conducting a search for an independent board director with defense industry or aerospace manufacturing experience. Target: appointment by Q3 2026.

4. **Apprenticeship Program:** The apprenticeship program manager reports to HR but works closely with the VP of Manufacturing on training curriculum and apprentice placement. The program currently has 35 active apprentices across Plant 1 and Plant 2.
