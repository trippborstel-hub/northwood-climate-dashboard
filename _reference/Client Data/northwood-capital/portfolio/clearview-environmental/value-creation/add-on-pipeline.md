## Completed Acquisition

### GreenTech Testing — Completed Q1 2024

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Target | GreenTech Testing LLC |
| Location | Mid-Atlantic (Maryland) |
| Acquisition Date | Q1 2024 |
| Enterprise Value | ~$28.0M |
| Entry Multiple | ~7.0x |
| Revenue at Acquisition | $18.0M |
| EBITDA at Acquisition | $4.0M |
| Employees | 45 |
| Rationale | PFAS testing specialist; EPA-certified analytical methods; proprietary sample prep technology |
| Funding | Revolver draw ($15M) + cash on hand ($13M); revolver subsequently repaid from operating cash flow |

**Post-Acquisition Performance:**
- All 45 employees retained (100% retention of certified lab technicians)
- EBITDA contribution of $4.0M (LTM), in line with underwrite
- Cross-sell of remediation services to GreenTech clients generated $2.8M incremental revenue
- PFAS lab throughput increased 60% through combined equipment and staffing
- Integration 85% complete; LIMS migration remaining (Q2 2026)

**Key Lesson:** PFAS testing demand was stronger than underwrite assumptions. The acquisition has been accretive from Day 1 and positioned Clearview as a PFAS testing leader in the Mid-Atlantic.

## Pipeline Selection Criteria

All add-on targets are evaluated against Clearview-specific criteria:

| Criterion | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Geography | Southeast, Texas/Gulf Coast, or West Coast (priority expansion regions) |
| Revenue | $10M-$40M |
| EBITDA | $2M+ |
| Entry Multiple | 5-8x (must be accretive to platform valuation) |
| Service Overlap | Environmental remediation, testing, compliance, or emergency response |
| Lab Certifications | Strongly preferred (EPA-certified analytical labs) |
| Customer Base | Diversified, no single customer >15% |
| Management | Retain key operators; founder exit acceptable if bench depth exists |
