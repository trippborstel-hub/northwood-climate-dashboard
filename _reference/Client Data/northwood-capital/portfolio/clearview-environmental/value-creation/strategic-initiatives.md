**Last Updated:** February 2026
**Owner:** Priya Kapoor (Deal Lead), David Okafor (Operating Partner)

## Value Creation Plan Overview

The Clearview value creation plan is built on four pillars: (1) PFAS testing expansion to capture a generational regulatory tailwind, (2) add-on acquisitions to build a national platform, (3) field operations capacity and efficiency improvements, and (4) geographic diversification to reduce regional concentration and access higher-growth markets.

**Target Outcome:** Exit at 9.5-10.5x EBITDA in Year 4-5 (2027-2028) with EBITDA of $42-50M, implying enterprise value of $400-525M and gross MOIC of 2.0-2.5x+.

## Initiative 1: PFAS Testing Expansion

**Status:** On Track
**RAG:** Green
**Owner:** Steven Park (COO), Lab Director

### Objective
Grow PFAS testing revenue from $35M (2025) to $55M+ by 2028, reaching 25%+ of total revenue. Establish Clearview as a top-3 regional PFAS testing provider.

### Rationale
EPA finalized PFAS MCLs in 2024, creating mandatory testing obligations across thousands of contaminated sites. 28 states have enacted or proposed PFAS regulations. Total addressable PFAS remediation and testing market estimated at $400B-$1T over the next 20+ years. This is non-discretionary, regulatory-driven demand.

### Key Actions

| Action | Timeline | Status | Impact |
|--------|----------|--------|--------|
| GreenTech Testing acquisition integration | Q1 2024 — Q2 2026 | 85% complete | Added PFAS analytical capabilities and 45 staff |
| PFAS lab capacity expansion (equipment) | Q2 2026 | Pending board approval | Increase throughput 40% |
| EPA Method 533 and 537.1 certifications | Ongoing | Certified in all 3 labs | Required for federal contract eligibility |
| State-level certification expansion | 2026-2027 | 18 of 28 target states | Broader geographic eligibility |
| Dedicated PFAS sales team (3 FTEs) | Q1 2026 | Hired 2, 1 open | Accelerate contract pipeline |

### KPIs

| Metric | 2024 | 2025 | 2026 Target | 2028 Target |
|--------|------|------|-------------|-------------|
| PFAS Revenue | $25.5M | $35.1M | $38-42M | $55M+ |
| PFAS % of Revenue | 15.0% | 18.5% | 18-20% | 25%+ |
| Active PFAS Contracts | 28 | 35 | 50 | 80 |
| PFAS Lab Throughput (samples/month) | 1,200 | 1,800 | 2,500 | 3,500 |

## Initiative 2: Add-on Acquisition Pipeline

**Status:** Active
**RAG:** Green
**Owner:** Priya Kapoor (Deal Lead)

### Objective
Complete 2-3 additional add-on acquisitions over the remaining hold period, adding $8-15M of incremental EBITDA at 5-7x acquisition multiples. Build geographic coverage across at least 4 major regions.

### Rationale
The environmental services market is highly fragmented ($50B+ market, thousands of regional operators). No dominant mid-market platform exists with national coverage. Add-on acquisitions at 5-7x create significant multiple arbitrage against the platform's 9.0-9.5x valuation.

|--------|------|-----|----------|--------|-----------|
