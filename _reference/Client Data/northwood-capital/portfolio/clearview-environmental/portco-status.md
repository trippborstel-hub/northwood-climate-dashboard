## Performance Summary

Clearview is tracking on plan across all key dimensions. Revenue growth has been driven by strong demand in environmental remediation and testing, supported by EPA PFAS enforcement actions and state-level environmental regulation. The GreenTech Testing add-on (closed Q1 2024 for approximately $28M) brought specialized PFAS testing capabilities and 45 employees, expanding Clearview's addressable market in the fastest-growing segment of environmental services.

EBITDA margins have held at 16.0%, consistent with entry levels. While the GreenTech integration introduced some near-term margin dilution (GreenTech operated at lower margins pre-acquisition), operational improvements and revenue scale have offset the impact. Field technician utilization is running at approximately 78%, above the 75% budget target.

The company has built a $42M project backlog as of January 2026, providing roughly five months of revenue visibility. PFAS-related testing revenue now represents approximately 18% of the total revenue mix, up from near-zero at entry. Recurring testing contracts account for approximately 35% of revenue, providing a stable base against the project-based remediation work.
