## Performance Summary

Pinnacle is 4 months post-close. Revenue of $115.0M (LTM) represents 6% growth over the entry run rate, driven by favorable project mix in Q4 2024 (refinery turnaround season) and modest pricing improvements. EBITDA margin has compressed 50 bps from 18.0% at entry to 17.5%, reflecting post-close integration costs (ERP assessment, safety program audit, organizational restructuring) and the Q1 seasonal dip in inspection demand.

The management transition has proceeded smoothly. Paul Stevens (formerly COO, now President) has established credibility with the team and key customers. Frank Morrison (CEO, external hire at close) is focused on strategic growth — add-on pipeline development, geographic expansion planning, and building the technician recruitment infrastructure.

At an 8.5x exit multiple on current EBITDA, the implied EV is $170.9M, generating a 1.2x gross MOIC on $93.0M of invested equity. IRR is not meaningful given the short hold period.
