## Commentary

Pinnacle is 4 months post-close and performing in line with expectations for this stage of ownership. Revenue is $1.5M below budget, driven by the typical Q1 maintenance season dip in refinery and pipeline inspection demand. This seasonal pattern was anticipated in the underwrite and is consistent with the industrial technology seasonality profile. The project backlog at $28.5M (3.0 months) provides reasonable near-term visibility.

The integration workstreams are progressing on schedule. The ERP assessment has been completed (recommended migration to a unified project management and billing system in 2026). The safety program audit identified 8 corrective actions, 6 of which have been completed. Technician utilization at 73% remains near the entry level — improvement initiatives (scheduling optimization, geographic crew balancing) are being designed and will launch in Q1 2026.

The most critical near-term priority is launching the technician recruitment pipeline and evaluating the first add-on acquisition target.
