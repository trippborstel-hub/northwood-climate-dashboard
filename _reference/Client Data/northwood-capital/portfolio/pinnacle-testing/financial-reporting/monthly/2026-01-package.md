## Executive Summary

January 2026 revenue of $8.5M was $0.7M below budget ($9.2M), reflecting the expected Q1 maintenance season dip in industrial inspection demand. Refinery turnaround season ended in December, and January-February is historically the lowest demand period as clients finalize budgets and maintenance schedules for the year. EBITDA of $1.3M was $0.2M below budget ($1.5M) with margin at 15.3% versus 16.3% budget, reflecting lower revenue absorption of fixed costs and ongoing integration expenses. These results are consistent with the seasonal pattern identified in diligence and are not cause for concern.

This is only the fourth monthly reporting package since close. Limited prior-year comparables are available as Pinnacle operated under the founder's less structured financial reporting prior to acquisition.
