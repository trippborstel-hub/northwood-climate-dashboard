## 4. Operations Update

### Service Delivery
- Billable hours up 6% YoY in Q4, driven by refinery turnaround volume and two large pipeline integrity projects
- Advanced NDT services (phased array, TOFD) grew 12% YoY — higher bill rates ($165-180/hr vs. $115-125/hr for conventional) contributing to gross margin expansion
- Infrastructure inspection (bridges, highways) continued to grow as IIJA-funded projects come online — now 15% of revenue, up from 12% at entry
- Q4 turnaround season execution was strong: zero safety incidents across 14 active turnaround sites

### Technology
- ERP assessment completed Day 14 — recommended migration to Procore or Viewpoint (field services / construction ERP)
- RFP to be issued in Q2 2026, implementation targeted for H2 2026
- Current systems (QuickBooks + Excel + Access database) remain a constraint on real-time project visibility and utilization tracking
- Interim solution: implemented standardized Excel-based project tracking templates across all crews to bridge gap until ERP migration

### Safety
- TRIR improved from 0.95 (at entry) to 0.88 (Q4 2025) and 0.82 (January 2026)
- Zero lost-time incidents since November 2024 (14 consecutive months as of Q4 board)
- Near-miss reporting program increasing report volume — positive cultural indicator
- Safety corrective action plan from Day 30 audit: 6 of 8 items complete; remaining 2 (confined space procedures, crane protocol) expected complete by end of February 2026
- Safety performance is becoming a competitive differentiator in customer bids — multiple RFPs weight safety record at 20-30% of evaluation criteria

### Human Capital
- 15 net new hires since close (260 to 275 as of January 2026)
- Inspector turnover improved from 16% to 14% (annualized)
- Average inspector age of 48 and Level III average of 54 remain a structural concern — technician pipeline program is the response
- Compensation benchmarking completed: Pinnacle is competitive for Gulf Coast but below national platform peers. 2026 budget includes targeted adjustments for Level III inspectors and regional leads
