## Summary

Keystone Behavioral Health is an ABA therapy platform acquired in February 2024. The company is in the 100-day plan execution phase, with workstreams focused on centralizing billing, migrating to a unified EHR system, and building a BCBA therapist recruitment pipeline. Early indicators are mixed — revenue growth is tracking at 10% since acquisition, but therapist recruitment is behind plan and EBITDA margin has compressed slightly from 17.8% at entry to 17.5% as the company invests in infrastructure. RAG status is Amber reflecting early-stage execution risk and the critical dependence on therapist pipeline development.

## Performance Summary

Keystone is approximately 24 months post-close and in the final stages of 100-day plan execution. Revenue has grown 10% from $125.0M to $138.0M, driven by patient volume increases and modest rate improvements on commercial payor contracts. EBITDA has grown 9% to $24.2M, though margin has compressed 30 bps to 17.5% as the company absorbs investment in centralized billing infrastructure, EHR migration costs, and incremental clinical leadership hires.

The company currently operates with 1,250 active patients and 340 employees, including approximately 85 Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs) and 180 Registered Behavior Technicians (RBTs). Payor mix is concentrated across three major payors: BlueCross (28%), UnitedHealth (22%), and Medicaid (18%).

The 100-day plan is progressing with some workstreams ahead of schedule (centralized billing) and others lagging (BCBA recruitment pipeline, payor renegotiations). Amber RAG status reflects the early-stage nature of the investment and the critical importance of therapist pipeline execution to the growth thesis.
