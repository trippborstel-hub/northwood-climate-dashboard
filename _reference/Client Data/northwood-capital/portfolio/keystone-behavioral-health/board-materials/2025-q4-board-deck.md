**Assessment:** Revenue and EBITDA are tracking 3-5% below the original IC underwrite, driven primarily by the BCBA hiring shortfall. The execution gap is in therapist recruitment, which constrains the speed of capacity expansion. The value creation plan has been recalibrated to reflect more realistic hiring timelines and a phased clinic expansion approach.

### Pipeline Initiatives

| Initiative | Status | Expected Impact |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| University partnerships (3 programs) | Active | 8 candidates graduating May/June 2026 |
| RBT-to-BCBA internal promotion | Active | 5 candidates expected to complete certification by Q3 2026 |
| Compensation benchmarking study | In progress (Feb delivery) | Market-competitive package to reduce offer declines |
| Signing bonus program | Proposed | $5-10K signing bonuses for experienced BCBAs |
| Clinical mentorship program | Active | Improved retention through career development |

### Competitive Landscape

The BCBA market remains extremely competitive. Large-platform ABA providers (LEARN, Trumpet Behavioral Health, Centria) are offering $85-100K base salaries, $10-15K signing bonuses, and equity participation. Keystone is currently at $78-92K base with no signing bonus. The compensation study will recommend adjustments to close this gap.

## 5. 2026 Clinic Expansion Plan

### Planned Openings

| Clinic | Market | Population (ASD) | Wait List Data | BCBA Supply | Open Target | Capex |
|--------|--------|-------------------|----------------|-------------|-------------|-------|
| Charlotte, NC | 2.7M MSA | High demand, 3 competitors | 8-month avg. | Moderate | Q2 2026 | $1.2M |

**Site Selection Methodology:** Markets ranked by autism prevalence, existing ABA supply gap (wait list data from state agencies), BCBA availability, reimbursement rates, and state regulatory environment.

**Contingency:** Raleigh opening is contingent on achieving BCBA hiring target of 15 net adds by end of Q2. If hiring is below 10, Raleigh will be deferred to 2027.
