## Executive Summary

January 2026 revenue of $11.2M came in $0.6M below budget ($11.8M), reflecting the expected seasonal Q1 dip from insurance deductible resets. Patient session volumes declined as families exhausted deductibles and co-pay obligations increased. EBITDA of $1.8M was $0.3M below budget ($2.1M), with margin at 16.1% versus 17.8% budget. The margin compression is typical for January and expected to normalize by March as deductible accumulation reduces out-of-pocket burden. Therapist hiring remains the primary area of concern — two BCBA offers were declined in January due to competitive counteroffers.
