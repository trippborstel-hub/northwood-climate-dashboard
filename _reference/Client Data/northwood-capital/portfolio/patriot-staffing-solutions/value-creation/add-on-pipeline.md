## Pipeline Pause Context

The add-on acquisition pipeline was formally paused in Q3 2025 when it became clear that Patriot's margin performance was trending below plan. The original value creation thesis called for 2-3 add-on acquisitions at 4-6x EBITDA over the first 3 years. As of February 2026 (2.5 years post-close), no add-ons have been completed.

**Rationale for Pausing:**
1. **Management bandwidth** — The margin recovery plan (bill rate renegotiation, workers' comp management, recruiter retention) requires focused attention from the CEO and COO. Executing an acquisition in parallel would dilute focus at a critical time.
2. **Financing constraints** — Below-plan EBITDA reduces incremental debt capacity. Current leverage of 2.6x (term) / 2.8x (total) is manageable, but adding acquisition debt while margins are compressing is imprudent.
3. **Integration risk** — Integrating an acquired staffing firm requires operational capacity (recruiting, payroll, client onboarding) that is already strained by current performance challenges.
4. **Narrative risk** — A PE-backed platform acquiring while its own margins are deteriorating sends a problematic signal to lenders, co-investors, and the acquired management team.

**Maintaining Relationships:** The deal team is maintaining periodic contact with the two highest-priority targets (Target 1 and Target 2) to preserve optionality for reactivation. Neither target is currently in a formal sale process.
