## Executive Team

### David Harrington — Chief Executive Officer

**Tenure:** Joined 2015 (as President), CEO since 2017
**Age:** 52
**Background:** 18 years in business services and outsourcing. Prior to Meridian, served as COO of a $150M national staffing platform backed by a mid-market PE firm, where he led operations through a period of 40% revenue growth and two add-on integrations. Earlier career included leadership roles at a Fortune 500 outsourcing company (8 years), progressing from regional operations manager to VP of Strategic Accounts. Joined Meridian as President in 2015 to professionalize operations ahead of a potential transaction, and was promoted to CEO in 2017 when the founder began transition planning.

**Education:** MBA, University of Michigan Ross School of Business; BA, Economics, Indiana University

**Key Strengths:** Deep client relationships, operational discipline, proven ability to scale services businesses through organic and acquisitive growth. Led Meridian through two successful add-on integrations and more than doubled revenue during tenure. Strong presence in buyer meetings and management presentations.

**Rollover/Equity:** Participated in management rollover at entry (20% pool). Fully aligned with exit outcome.

### Patricia Wells — Chief Financial Officer

**Tenure:** Joined 2016
**Age:** 48
**Background:** 15 years in finance and accounting. Previously VP of Finance at a PE-backed business services company ($120M revenue), where she led financial planning, reporting, and compliance through a successful exit process. Earlier career at a Big Four accounting firm (PwC, 6 years) in the assurance practice, specializing in services and outsourcing clients. Licensed CPA.

**Education:** MBA, University of Illinois Gies College of Business; BS, Accounting, University of Wisconsin

Experienced in PE reporting cadence and exit preparation (vendor due diligence, data room construction). Viewed as a high-quality CFO by the Northwood deal team.

**Rollover/Equity:** Participated in management equity pool. Aligned with exit.
