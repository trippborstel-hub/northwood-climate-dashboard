## 1. Executive Summary

Meridian delivered strong Q4 2025 results, capping a year that exceeded plan on all key financial and operating metrics. Full-year 2025 revenue of $222.5M (+8.5% YoY) and EBITDA of $37.7M (+10.2%) reflect continued organic growth momentum and margin expansion. The business is well-positioned for exit, with sell-side advisor selection targeted for Q1 2026 and process launch in H2 2026.

**Key Headlines:**
- Q4 revenue of $58.5M (+9.0% YoY), EBITDA of $10.2M (+11.5% YoY)
- Full-year EBITDA margin of 16.9%, up 20 bps from prior year
- Net leverage at 1.1x — lowest since acquisition
- 12 new customer wins in 2025 (net +13 including attrition)
- 2026 budget approved: $243.0M revenue, $42.2M EBITDA
- Exit preparation on track — sell-side advisor RFP in progress

### Sell-Side Advisor RFP
RFP distributed to three investment banks with strong BPO/staffing sector coverage. Presentations scheduled for January-February 2026. Key selection criteria: sector expertise, buyer relationships (strategic + sponsor), execution track record, valuation perspective.

### Buyer Universe (Preliminary)
- **Strategics:** Large BPO platforms seeking managed staffing capabilities (3-4 targets identified)
- **Financial sponsors:** Larger PE funds seeking scaled BPO platforms (5-6 targets identified)
- **Expected competitive dynamics:** Scaled, profitable BPO platforms with 17%+ margins and strong management teams are rare in the market. Expect strong buyer interest.

### Valuation Perspective
At 9.5x on current EBITDA of $38.3M, the implied EV is $363.9M. If 2026 EBITDA tracks to budget ($42.2M), exit on forward EBITDA could yield $401.0M EV. Expect competitive tension to support 9.0-10.0x range.
