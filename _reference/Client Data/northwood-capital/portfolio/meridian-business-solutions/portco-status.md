## Performance Summary

Meridian has materially outperformed the original underwrite on all dimensions. Revenue has grown 73% since acquisition ($130.0M to $225.0M), driven by two successful add-on acquisitions (Workforce Plus, Q2 2019; TalentBridge, Q4 2020) and organic growth across the mid-market BPO segment. EBITDA margin has expanded 30 bps to 17.0% through shared services consolidation and procurement savings post-integration. The company has paid down $36.0M of debt (from $78.0M to $42.0M) and returned $30.0M in distributions ($20.0M dividend recap in Q2 2022, $10.0M cumulative tax distributions).

At a 9.5x exit multiple on current EBITDA, the implied EV is $363.9M, generating a 3.0x gross MOIC on $117.0M of invested equity.
