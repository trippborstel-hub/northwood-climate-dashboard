## Current Pipeline Status

**Pipeline activity is paused pending exit process launch.**

As Meridian enters exit preparation, the decision has been made to pause active add-on pursuit. This is driven by three considerations:

1. **Narrative simplicity:** Buyers prefer to evaluate a clean, stable business without in-flight integration risk
2. **Management bandwidth:** CEO and CFO bandwidth will be partially allocated to the exit process (management presentations, buyer meetings, due diligence)
3. **Optionality for buyers:** Unexecuted pipeline targets can be presented to buyers as identified growth opportunities, potentially supporting a higher exit valuation

## Add-on Track Record — Exit Narrative

The completed add-on program demonstrates Northwood's and Meridian management's ability to:

1. **Source** — Identified 15+ targets through proprietary and intermediary channels
2. **Evaluate** — Maintained disciplined entry multiples (5.9-6.6x) well below platform valuation
3. **Integrate** — Completed both integrations within 12-15 months with documented playbook
4. **Cross-sell** — Drove significant revenue synergies ($7.5M+ incremental revenue from cross-sell)
5. **Scale** — Combined add-on revenue grew from $27M at acquisition to $46M current (70% growth)

This track record, combined with the identified pipeline of additional targets, should be a meaningful component of the exit story — particularly for financial sponsor buyers who will underwrite further add-on-driven growth.
