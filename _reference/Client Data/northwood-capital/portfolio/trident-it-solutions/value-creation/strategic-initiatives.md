## 1. Cybersecurity Revenue Growth

### Strategy

Cybersecurity is the primary growth engine and the core differentiator of the Trident platform. The strategy has three prongs:

1. **Cross-sell to existing MSP customers:** 52% of Trident's managed services customers now also purchase cybersecurity services, up from 0% at entry. Target: 65% penetration by YE 2026, 80% by YE 2027.

2. **Cyber-led new logo acquisition:** An increasing number of new customers are arriving through cybersecurity (vs. MSP) as the entry point. In 2025, 40% of new logos were cyber-led (vs. 25% in 2024).

3. **Service expansion:** Expand the cybersecurity offering from detection and response to proactive services — threat hunting, red team assessments, cyber insurance readiness, and security awareness training.

### Execution Progress

| Metric | At Entry (Jan 2021) | Current (Jan 2026) | 2026 Target |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|-------------|
| Cybersecurity Revenue | $0 | $35.5M (LTM) | $42.0M |
| % of Total Revenue | 0% | 25% | 27% |
| Cybersecurity Customers | 0 | 62 | 75 |
| SOC Analysts | 0 | 28 | 32 |
| Cross-Sell Rate | N/A | 52% | 65% |
| Cyber Revenue per Customer | N/A | $573K | $560K |

### Cross-Sell Playbook

The cybersecurity cross-sell motion follows a proven sequence:

1. **Security Assessment** (free or low-cost): Identify gaps in existing MSP customer's security posture
2. **Quick Win Implementation** (vulnerability scanning, endpoint protection): $5-10K/month — low barrier to entry
3. **MDR / SOC-as-a-Service:** $15-25K/month — the core cybersecurity engagement
4. **Compliance Monitoring:** $8-15K/month add-on for regulated industries (healthcare, financial services)
5. **Incident Response Retainer:** $3-5K/month — insurance policy for the customer

Average time from initial assessment to full cybersecurity engagement: 4-6 months.

## 3. Talent Retention Program

### Challenge

Cybersecurity talent is the single most critical constraint on Trident's growth. The cybersecurity labor market remains extremely competitive — SOC analysts, security engineers, and incident responders are in demand across all industries and geographies. Trident's cybersecurity staff turnover peaked at 22% in 2024, materially above the company-wide average of 16%.

### Program Design (Launched Q4 2025)

| Component | Investment | Details |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Retention equity grants | $1.2M (one-time) | 2-year cliff vesting for top 15 cybersecurity performers; aligns with exit timeline |
| Career ladder (SOC I-II-III-Lead-Manager) | $0.1M/yr | Defined skill requirements, comp bands, and promotion criteria at each level |
| Certification reimbursement | $0.2M/yr | Full reimbursement for CISSP, CEH, OSCP, GIAC certifications + $5K completion bonus |
| University pipeline program | $0.1M/yr | Partnerships with 2 universities; 8-10 cybersecurity interns/year; 60% intern-to-hire target |
| Remote work flexibility | Minimal cost | SOC analysts can work 2 remote shifts/week (24/7 SOC requires on-site coverage for 5 shifts) |
| Annual cybersecurity conference budget | $0.15M/yr | Send 10 staff to DEF CON, Black Hat, RSA; professional development + retention |

### Early Results (Q4 2025 vs. Q3 2025)

| Metric | Q3 2025 | Q4 2025 | Trend |
|--------|---------|---------|-------|
| Cybersecurity annualized voluntary turnover | 20% | 15% | Improving |

| Average time to fill (cyber roles) | 62 days | 48 days | Improving |

## 5. CMMC Certification

### Background

The Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC) is a DoD requirement for all defense contractors and their IT service providers. Trident's pursuit of CMMC Level 2 certification opens a significant new market — mid-market defense contractors who need CMMC-compliant IT managed services and cybersecurity.

### Timeline

| Milestone | Target Date | Status |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Gap assessment completed | Q4 2025 | Complete |
| Remediation plan developed | Q1 2026 | Complete |
| Control implementation | Q1-Q2 2026 | In progress |
| Assessment readiness review | March 2026 | Scheduled |
| Third-party assessment | Q2-Q3 2026 | TBD |
| Certification | Q3 2026 | Target |

### Market Opportunity

| Segment | Estimated TAM |
|---------|--------------|
| Mid-market defense contractors needing CMMC-compliant IT | $8M |
| Government agencies needing compliant managed services | $5M |
| Defense supply chain companies (sub-tier) | $3M |
| **Total Addressable (Year 1-2 post-certification)** | **~$15M** |

CMMC certification also creates a competitive moat — many mid-market MSPs lack the investment capacity to achieve certification, limiting competition in this segment.
