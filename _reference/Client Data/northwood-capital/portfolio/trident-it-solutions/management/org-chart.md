## Organizational Notes

1. **SOC Structure:** The Security Operations Center operates 24/7/365 across two locations (Midwest HQ and Chicago). The SOC is organized in three tiers: Tier 1 (alert triage, 12 analysts), Tier 2 (investigation, 8 analysts), and Tier 3 (advanced threat hunting and incident response, 8 analysts + the SOC Lead). A Tier 1 automation initiative (part of platform modernization Phase II) is expected to reduce the number of Tier 1 analysts required and shift headcount toward higher-value Tier 2/3 activities.

2. **COO Role:** James Rivera was hired in February 2022, just months before the ShieldPoint acquisition, specifically to manage the increased operational complexity of integrating an MSSP. He oversees all service delivery (NOC, SOC, field services, professional services) and is the primary owner of the platform modernization initiative. His Rackspace experience managing large-scale managed services operations has been directly applicable.

3. **Sales Reorganization (2025):** The sales team was reorganized in Q3 2025 to add dedicated cybersecurity sales specialists (3 positions). Previously, enterprise AEs were responsible for selling the full stack (MSP + cyber). The reorganization reflects the increasing complexity of cybersecurity sales cycles and the need for domain expertise in customer conversations. Early results are positive — cyber-led new logo wins increased from 25% to 40% of total new logos.

4. **NetWatch Integration:** The Charlotte office operates semi-autonomously for local service delivery but reports through the VP of Service Delivery. Back-office functions (finance, HR, IT) were consolidated to HQ within 6 months of acquisition. A local account management team maintains customer relationships.

5. **Founder Transition:** The original founder served as a Senior Advisor for 18 months post-close (through mid-2022), supporting customer relationship transitions and strategic introductions. He has since fully transitioned out. His board observer role ended December 2022. No ongoing engagement.
