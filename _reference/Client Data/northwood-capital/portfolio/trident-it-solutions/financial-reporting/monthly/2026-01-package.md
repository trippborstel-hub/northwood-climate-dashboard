## Executive Summary

January performance was solid and in line with expectations for the seasonally softest month. Revenue of $11.5M was slightly below monthly budget ($11.6M), a minor variance driven by two professional services project delays. Recurring revenue (MRR) grew to $10.3M, up from $10.1M in December, reflecting three new cybersecurity contract activations. EBITDA margin of 16.8% was slightly below budget (17.0%) due to the timing of SOC hiring (two analysts started January vs. budgeted for March). No material concerns.
