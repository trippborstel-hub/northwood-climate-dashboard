## Risks to Budget

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Cybersecurity talent attrition | Medium-High | Key SOC analysts lost = service degradation | Retention equity, competitive comp, career ladders, university pipeline |
| Contract renewal miss | Low | 1pp shortfall = $1.2M revenue risk | 96.5% historical rate; customer success team proactive engagement |
| Platform modernization delay | Medium | Service quality and analyst productivity impact | Phased approach; core NOC unaffected; parallel running of old/new systems |
| Economic slowdown affecting mid-market IT budgets | Low-Medium | Customer budget cuts, project deferrals | Recurring revenue provides floor; cybersecurity is non-discretionary |
| Pricing pressure from larger MSP competitors | Low | ACV compression on new wins | Differentiation through cybersecurity bundle; focus on customer outcomes |
