## Performance Summary

Trident has outperformed the original underwrite on revenue growth and platform buildout, driven by two successful add-on acquisitions and strong organic expansion. Revenue has grown 67% from $85.0M to $142.0M. The ShieldPoint Cyber acquisition (Q3 2022) added managed security services (MSSP) capability, which has become the fastest-growing segment at approximately 30% YoY growth. The NetWatch MSP acquisition (Q2 2023) extended geographic coverage and added 40 mid-market managed services customers.

EBITDA margin has compressed 140 bps from 18.9% to 17.5%, reflecting the addition of lower-margin add-on businesses and elevated investment in cybersecurity talent. On a like-for-like basis (excluding add-on dilution), legacy Trident operations have maintained margins above 20%. Management projects margin re-expansion to 18.5%+ as cybersecurity contracts mature and scale efficiencies are realized.

The company has paid down $30.0M of debt and returned $12.0M in cumulative tax distributions. Recurring revenue exceeds 85% of total, and the customer base is well-diversified with the top customer representing only 8% of revenue.
