## Executive Summary

January 2026 was a seasonally soft month, consistent with historical patterns. Revenue of $7.5M came in slightly below the January budget of $7.7M, primarily due to delayed starts on two tenant improvement projects in Denver and Salt Lake City (weather-related, not client-driven). PM fee revenue was on plan. EBITDA of $1.3M was modestly below budget, reflecting the revenue shortfall. The managed portfolio remained stable at 18.0M sq ft with no mandate losses. The TI project pipeline provides confidence in a solid Q2 ramp.

**Month RAG: Green** — on plan for seasonal period; shortfall is timing-related.
