## Northwood Recommendations

1. **Client relationship transition — highest priority.** Complete the remaining 6 of 20 joint client meetings (Drummond + Webb) by Q2 2026. Develop a structured framework for transferring primary client ownership from Drummond to Webb and Torres over the next 12-18 months. Monitor client satisfaction through the transition.
2. **CTO hire — critical path.** The technology platform decision cannot be made without senior technical leadership. CTO search is underway with a Q3 2026 target start date. Ensure competitive compensation ($250-300K total comp) and equity participation to attract candidates with multi-tenant SaaS platform experience.
3. **Retention packages for Osei, Morales, Chen.** These three individuals represent the highest-concentration operational, technical, and technology roles. Ensure equity incentives are competitive and review annually. Chen's retention through at least Year 2 is particularly critical given the technology platform transition.
4. **Independent board director recruitment.** Currently an open seat. Recommend targeting a candidate with commercial real estate services experience — ideally someone with CRE technology or institutional property management background — to support the strategic growth agenda.
