**Plan Initiated:** July 1, 2025 (Day 1 = Close Date)
**Plan Owner:** Marcus Webb, COO (with oversight from Nadia Fernandez, Northwood VP / Deal Lead, and external real estate operating advisor)

## Plan Overview

The 100-day plan establishes the operational foundation for Ridgeline's transformation from a founder-led regional property management firm to a scalable multi-market platform capable of supporting 40-50M+ sq ft under management. The plan focuses on five critical workstreams: technology platform assessment, client relationship transition (Drummond de-risking), Las Vegas market build-out, institutional client pipeline development, and sustainability services opportunity scoping.

**Note:** While the calendar has moved well past 100 days from close, this document tracks the original 100-day plan milestones and their actual completion status. Some workstreams that were initiated within the 100-day window remain in execution phase.

## Workstream 1: Technology Platform Assessment (Day 60)

**Owner:** Robert Chen (Director of Technology), Christine Albright (CFO — interim technology oversight)
**Target:** Evaluate scalability of proprietary RidgelineOS platform and determine build vs. buy strategy
**Status:** IN PROGRESS

| Milestone | Target | Actual | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|--------|
| Technology audit (current state assessment) | Day 15 | Day 12 | Complete |
| Architecture review — scalability analysis | Day 30 | Day 28 | Complete |
| Commercial platform evaluation (Yardi, MRI, RealPage) | Day 45 | Day 50 | Complete |
| Vendor shortlist and cost analysis | Day 60 | Day 65 | Complete |
| Build vs. buy recommendation | Day 75 | Feb 2026 (est.) | In progress |
| CTO job spec and search firm engagement | Day 30 | Day 35 | Complete |
| CTO hire | Day 90 | Q3 2026 (est.) | In progress |
| Board presentation — technology decision | Day 100 | April 2026 (board mtg) | Scheduled |

**Assessment Findings:**
- RidgelineOS is functional and well-regarded by clients at current scale (18M sq ft, 7 offices). Tenant satisfaction with the platform is 4.3/5.0.
- Architecture is monolithic — built for a single-region operation. Scaling to 40M+ sq ft across multiple regions would require significant refactoring.
- The 4-person development team lacks experience with multi-tenant, multi-region SaaS architecture.
- Three commercial platform options evaluated: Yardi Voyager, MRI Software, and RealPage. All proven at scale but would sacrifice the custom client experience that differentiates Ridgeline.
- Hybrid option (Option C) is emerging as the leading candidate: retain RidgelineOS as the client-facing layer while migrating the back-end to a commercial platform.

**Key Decision:** Deferred to CTO hire (Q3 2026) and April board meeting for strategic direction. Estimated investment: $2.0-3.5M over 18-24 months depending on path chosen.

## Key Lessons Learned

1. **Las Vegas was the fastest win.** A pre-close market entry with strong pipeline development generated 3 mandates and $2.8M annualized revenue within 6 months. The playbook — Drummond's network for introductions, Torres for structured BD, Webb for operational credibility — is replicable for future market entries.
2. **Client transitions require more time than planned.** The founder-client relationships are deeply personal, built over 10-15+ years. Joint meetings are necessary but not sufficient — clients need to see Webb and Torres perform independently on real operational issues before they will fully transfer trust. Budget 18 months, not 90 days.
3. **Technology is the gating factor for scale.** The RidgelineOS platform is a competitive advantage at current scale but a constraint for growth. The build vs. buy decision cannot be deferred beyond Q3 2026 without affecting the add-on acquisition timeline. CTO hire is critical path.
4. **Sustainability services are an emerging strategic theme.** Client demand is real and growing. Building performance standards in Denver and Phoenix could make this urgent rather than optional within 12-18 months. Early mover advantage is available if Ridgeline invests now.
