## Business Description

NFC operates a technology-enabled freight brokerage connecting shippers with carriers across the continental United States. The Company's proprietary transportation management system (TMS) provides real-time load matching, rate optimization, and shipment visibility. NFC specializes in less-than-truckload (LTL) and full truckload (FTL) brokerage for mid-market shippers in the manufacturing, retail, and consumer products sectors.

---

## Investment Highlights

1. **Asset-light model** — No fleet ownership. Revenue generated from brokerage spread between shipper rates and carrier costs.
2. **Proprietary technology platform** — TMS built in-house over 8 years. Competitive advantage in load matching and rate optimization.
3. **Growing end market** — U.S. freight brokerage market ~$90B, growing 5–7% annually.
4. **Strong management team** — Experienced leadership with deep logistics industry relationships.
