## What We Liked

- **IT staffing is a strong subsector:** Growing demand for technology talent, recurring client relationships, and a fragmented competitive landscape. IT staffing is one of our preferred business services sub-sectors.
- **Decent margins for staffing:** 12.5% EBITDA margin is above the typical 7–10% range for staffing businesses, suggesting either favorable bill/pay rate spreads, efficient operations, or a higher-value service mix (more direct placement relative to contract staffing).
- **Southeast IT market exposure:** Atlanta, Charlotte, and Raleigh are among the fastest-growing technology employment markets in the U.S., providing favorable demand dynamics.

---

## What Killed It

At $8.5M EBITDA, TechTalent Staffing is below our $10.0M minimum for a platform investment. The business is too small for our equity check size and value creation model. Our control buyout approach requires sufficient scale to support institutional management costs, fund add-on acquisitions, and generate the absolute dollar returns our LPs expect.
