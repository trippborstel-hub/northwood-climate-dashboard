## Business Description

TechTalent Staffing provides IT staffing solutions including contract staffing, contract-to-hire, and direct placement for technology roles. The Company places software developers, cloud engineers, cybersecurity professionals, data analysts, and IT support personnel with mid-market clients. TTS operates from offices in Atlanta, Charlotte, and Raleigh.

---

## Investment Highlights

1. **Strong niche position** — Focused on IT staffing for mid-market clients, a segment underserved by national staffing firms.
2. **Attractive margins for staffing** — 12.5% EBITDA margin is above-average for the staffing sector.
3. **Growing end market** — IT labor demand continues to outpace supply, driving sustained need for staffing services.
4. **Experienced management team** — Founder/CEO with 20+ years in IT staffing.
