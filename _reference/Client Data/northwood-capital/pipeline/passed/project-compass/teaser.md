## Business Description

MedBill Solutions provides end-to-end revenue cycle management services to healthcare providers, combining a proprietary SaaS platform with managed services for claims processing, coding, denial management, and patient collections. The Company processes over 8 million claims annually and manages approximately $2.5 billion in gross collections for its clients.

---

## Investment Highlights

1. **High recurring revenue** — 95% of revenue is recurring under multi-year contracts with healthcare providers.
2. **Strong client retention** — 97% annual client retention rate over the past five years.
3. **Technology platform** — Proprietary RCM software with automation capabilities (AI-assisted coding, automated denial management) driving operational efficiency.
4. **Scalable model** — Technology leverage enables margin expansion as claim volume grows without proportional headcount increase.
