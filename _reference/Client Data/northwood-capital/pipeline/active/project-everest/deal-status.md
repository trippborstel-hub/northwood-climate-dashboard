## Deal Team Notes

- LOI submitted February 10, 2026 at $155.0M EV (8.6x), midpoint of the indicated range

- Exclusivity decision expected from Baird by February 25, 2026

- Working capital intensity ($35M+ inventory) is a flagged item — requires detailed cash conversion cycle analysis in QofE
- Founder/CEO retirement is the transaction driver — no internal succession plan identified
