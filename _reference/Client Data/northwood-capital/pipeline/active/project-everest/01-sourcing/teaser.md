## Investment Highlights

Baird has been retained as the exclusive financial advisor to **Specialty Chemical Solutions Inc.** ("SCS" or the "Company") in connection with a potential sale transaction. The Company's founder and majority shareholder is pursuing a full exit as part of a planned retirement.

---

## Company Overview

Specialty Chemical Solutions is a leading regional distributor of specialty and performance chemicals, headquartered in **Cincinnati, Ohio**. **Founded in 1985** by Richard Hoffman, the Company has operated continuously for over 40 years, serving industrial and commercial customers across the Midwest and Mid-Atlantic regions.

SCS provides formulated specialty chemicals, performance additives, and custom-blended products to customers in the water treatment, metalworking, food & beverage processing, agriculture, and institutional cleaning sectors. The Company operates from a 150,000 sq ft headquarters and distribution center in Cincinnati with three additional regional distribution warehouses strategically located across its service territory.

The Company is distinguished by its technical application expertise, custom blending capabilities, and long-standing customer relationships — the average customer tenure exceeds 15 years, and no customer has been lost to a competitor in the past 5 years.

---

## Key Investment Highlights

- **Essential Products with Inelastic Demand:** SCS distributes critical process chemicals that are consumed in customers' daily operations. Chemical inputs represent a small fraction of customers' total cost of goods sold (<3% typically) but are essential to manufacturing processes, water treatment, and food safety — creating low price sensitivity and high retention.

- **40-Year Customer Relationships with 85% Retention Rate:** The Company has cultivated deeply embedded customer relationships over four decades. The top 20 customers have been purchasing from SCS for an average of 18 years. Retention is driven by technical application support, custom blending, and supply chain reliability.

- **Fragmented Market with Consolidation Opportunity:** The U.S. specialty chemical distribution market is highly fragmented with hundreds of regional distributors. SCS has identified 5 potential acquisition targets in adjacent geographies that would expand the Company's reach and product breadth.

- **Diversified End Markets:** Revenue is distributed across five core end markets — no single end market exceeds 30% of revenue. This diversification provides resilience through economic cycles.

- **Experienced Management Team:** The Company's leadership team has a combined 65+ years of industry experience. CFO David Schmidt and VP Sales Karen Walsh are expected to continue post-transaction.
