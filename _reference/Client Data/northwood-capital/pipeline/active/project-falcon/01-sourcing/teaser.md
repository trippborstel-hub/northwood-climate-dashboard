## Opportunity Overview

Houlihan Lokey has been retained to advise on the sale of **National Environmental Testing Labs** ("NETL" or the "Company"), a leading provider of environmental testing, analytical laboratory services, and remediation support to industrial, energy, and municipal clients across the Gulf Coast and Southern United States.

## Business Description

NETL is a full-service environmental testing and analytical services provider serving industrial, energy, and municipal clients. The Company operates six laboratory facilities strategically positioned across the Gulf Coast region, providing a comprehensive suite of services:

**Core Service Lines:**

- **Environmental Analytical Testing (65% of revenue)** — Soil, water, and air sample analysis for regulatory compliance. Testing for contaminants including PFAS, heavy metals, VOCs, and petroleum hydrocarbons. Accredited under NELAP and ISO 17025 standards.

- **Remediation Support Services (20% of revenue)** — Sampling, monitoring, and analytical support for environmental remediation projects. Ongoing monitoring contracts for brownfield sites, petroleum storage facilities, and industrial discharge compliance.

- **Emerging Contaminant Testing (15% of revenue)** — Specialized testing for PFAS compounds, microplastics, and emerging contaminants of concern. Fastest-growing segment, driven by new EPA regulations and state-level requirements.

## Market Opportunity

The U.S. environmental testing market is a **~$12 billion** industry experiencing sustained growth driven by:

- **Federal regulatory expansion** — EPA PFAS drinking water standards (effective 2024), increased enforcement of Clean Water Act and RCRA requirements, infrastructure spending creating environmental assessment demand.
- **State-level regulatory momentum** — 30+ states have enacted or proposed PFAS-related legislation. State environmental agencies expanding testing requirements for industrial and municipal clients.
- **Industrial compliance requirements** — Energy, manufacturing, and chemical companies face increasing environmental reporting obligations and remediation mandates.
- **Market fragmentation** — The environmental testing market remains highly fragmented, with hundreds of regional laboratories. The top 10 players account for less than 25% of the market, creating a significant consolidation opportunity.

## Investment Highlights

1. **Attractive recurring revenue model** — 80% of revenue derived from recurring testing contracts with multi-year visibility. Environmental compliance testing is non-discretionary and regulated.

2. **Powerful regulatory tailwinds** — EPA PFAS standards, state-level environmental regulations, and infrastructure spending are driving sustained demand growth. NETL's PFAS testing capabilities position the Company at the forefront of a multi-year growth cycle.

3. **Fragmented market with consolidation opportunity** — The $12B environmental testing market supports a platform acquisition strategy. 10+ regional laboratories have been identified as potential tuck-in acquisition targets.

4. **Proven management team** — Founder/CEO Tom Bradley has built the Company over 28 years. Experienced management team with deep technical expertise and customer relationships. CFO Lisa Nakamura joined in 2019 from a larger environmental services firm and has professionalized the financial function.

5. **Scalable laboratory infrastructure** — Six accredited laboratories with capacity for 30%+ volume growth without significant capital expenditure. Laboratory accreditation and certification requirements create meaningful barriers to entry.
