## Investment Highlights

Raymond James has been retained as the exclusive financial advisor to **CloudFirst Technologies** ("CloudFirst" or the "Company") in connection with a potential transaction. The Company's founder and majority shareholder is seeking a capital partner to support the next phase of growth while providing partial liquidity.

---

## Company Overview

CloudFirst Technologies is a leading provider of IT managed services and cybersecurity solutions to mid-market enterprises. Headquartered in **Atlanta, Georgia**, the Company was **founded in 2008** by James Whitfield and has grown into a trusted technology partner serving **118 clients** across the Southeastern United States.

The Company delivers comprehensive IT infrastructure management, cloud migration services, network monitoring, and managed cybersecurity through a highly recurring, contract-based model. CloudFirst has built deep expertise in regulated industries including financial services, healthcare, and professional services where IT reliability and security are mission-critical.

---

## Key Investment Highlights

- **Highly Recurring Revenue Model:** 85% of revenue is derived from multi-year managed services contracts with an average term of 3 years. Net revenue retention (NRR) of 112% demonstrates strong expansion within the existing customer base.

- **Dual Growth Engine — Managed Services + Cybersecurity:** The Company's cybersecurity practice, launched in 2019, is the fastest-growing segment and now represents approximately 25% of revenue. Cybersecurity is being cross-sold into the existing managed services customer base with strong attach rates.

- **Attractive and Growing End Market:** The U.S. managed IT services market exceeds $150 billion and is growing 8-10% annually, driven by increasing IT complexity, cybersecurity threats, and the structural shift of mid-market enterprises toward outsourced IT management.

- **Fragmented Market with Consolidation Opportunity:** The IT managed services sector is highly fragmented with thousands of sub-scale providers. The Company has identified 3 add-on acquisition targets that would accelerate geographic expansion and add cybersecurity capabilities.

- **Experienced Management Team:** Founder/CEO James Whitfield has led the Company for 17 years and is willing to roll approximately 20% of his equity into the transaction, ensuring alignment with the incoming capital partner. The management team includes an experienced CFO and a CTO with deep cybersecurity expertise.
