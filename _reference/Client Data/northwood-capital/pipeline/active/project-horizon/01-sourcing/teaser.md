## Opportunity Overview

William Blair has been retained to advise on the sale of **Smile Partners Management** ("SPM" or the "Company"), a dental practice management company operating as a Dental Support Organization (DSO) across 15 locations in the Southeastern United States.

The Company's current private equity sponsor is seeking to complete a successful exit after a five-year hold period, during which SPM has grown from 7 to 15 locations, more than doubled revenue, and established a proven de novo expansion model. The management team, led by CEO Dr. Katherine Reeves, intends to roll a meaningful portion of their equity into the next partnership.

## Business Description

Smile Partners Management operates a dental support organization model, providing non-clinical management services to affiliated dental practices. The Company handles practice management, billing and revenue cycle management, marketing, procurement, HR, and facility management — enabling dentists to focus on clinical care while benefiting from the operational scale and support of an institutional platform.

**Service Model:**

- **General Dentistry (70% of revenue)** — Preventive care, restorative procedures, and diagnostic services. The foundation of patient volume and recurring revenue.
- **Specialty Services (20% of revenue)** — Orthodontics, oral surgery, endodontics, and periodontics. Provided through in-house specialists and referral network partnerships.
- **Cosmetic and Elective (10% of revenue)** — Teeth whitening, veneers, and elective procedures. Higher margin, growth opportunity.

## Investment Highlights

1. **Proven DSO platform with demonstrated scalability** — Grown from 7 to 15 locations under current ownership with a repeatable de novo expansion model.

2. **Strong patient volume growth** — 12% YoY patient visit growth driven by new location ramp and same-store performance.

3. **Attractive expansion pipeline** — Three adjacent MSAs identified for near-term expansion (Memphis, Knoxville, Chattanooga), with site selection and lease negotiations underway.

4. **Experienced management team** — CEO Dr. Katherine Reeves has deep DSO operational experience. CFO Andrew Chen brings institutional financial discipline. COO Maria Gonzalez leads operations across all 15 locations.

5. **Non-discretionary healthcare demand** — Dental services benefit from predictable, recurring patient demand with favorable demographic trends.
