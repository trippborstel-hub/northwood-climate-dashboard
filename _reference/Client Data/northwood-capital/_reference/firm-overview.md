## Firm Description

Northwood Capital is a Chicago-based private equity firm focused on mid-market control buyouts in Business Services, Healthcare Services, Industrial Technology, Food & Agriculture, and Real Estate Services. Founded in 2014 by James Harrison and Catherine Zhao, the firm targets platform investments with enterprise values of $75M–$250M where operational improvement, add-on acquisitions, and management professionalization can drive value creation. Northwood acquires controlling positions (80%+ ownership) and works closely with management teams to build scalable, institutional-quality businesses.

The firm manages approximately $1.75B in total assets across two institutional funds. Fund I ($650M, 2017 vintage) is in harvest mode with a strong track record including two realized exits. Fund II ($1.1B, 2022 vintage) is actively deploying capital with six platform investments completed and capacity for additional acquisitions.

## LP Base

Northwood Capital's investor base consists of approximately 45 institutional limited partners. The LP composition reflects the firm's focus on building long-term institutional relationships and has remained stable across both fund cycles, with approximately 85% of Fund I LPs re-investing in Fund II.

| LP Category | Allocation | Representative Profile |
|---|---|---|
| **Public & Corporate Pension Funds** | ~40% | State pension systems, municipal retirement funds, and corporate defined-benefit plans. Largest single LP category. Includes allocations from Midwest and West Coast pension systems. |
| **Endowments & Foundations** | ~25% | University endowments (primarily mid-tier research universities) and private foundations. Attracted by Northwood's disciplined approach to mid-market buyouts and consistent return profile. |
| **Fund-of-Funds** | ~20% | Institutional fund-of-funds and private equity advisory firms that invest on behalf of smaller institutional clients. Provides diversified access to multiple LP segments. |
| **Family Offices** | ~10% | Multi-generational family offices, primarily Midwest and East Coast based. Several family offices have direct operational experience in Northwood's target sectors. |
| **GP Commitment** | ~5% | General partner commitment from Northwood partners and senior professionals. $13.0M in Fund I, $22.0M in Fund II. Demonstrates alignment of interest. |

**Advisory Board:** The Fund Advisory Board consists of five LP representatives who meet semi-annually to review conflicts of interest, fund expenses, valuation matters, and co-investment allocations.
