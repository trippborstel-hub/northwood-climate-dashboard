Year-by-Year Aggregate Budget $k
Methodology. LCOA = levelized cost over lever lifetime at the discount rate, net of energy savings, before carbon price. Target Optimization finds the minimum-cost lever set meeting the SBTi target at the planning horizon (greedy add in LCOA order, then trim overshoot). Exit Value Optimization selects levers with simple payback ≤ hold period. Efficiency levers reduce the load renewable procurement then fills (no double-count). Roll-up views auto-apply Target Optimization to each portco. Maintenance capex defaults: 1.5% of revenue (manufacturing/industrial), 1.0% (services/healthcare), 0.8% (staffing). Harvest Ridge and Ridgeline baselines are Kith estimates pending data collection.
