Methodology notes. Baseline emissions (S1=4,200 / S2=3,100 tCO2e) sourced from bau-targets.html. LCOA computed as levelized cost over lever lifetime at the user discount rate, net of energy savings, before any carbon price. Payback is simple (capex ÷ |annual opex savings|). The "Min Compliance" preset selects lowest-LCOA levers until the cumulative abatement closes the gap to the target line in the target year. "NPV-Optimal" selects all levers with negative or near-zero LCOA. "Exit-Value Max" selects levers whose payback fits within the horizon and whose opex savings flow to EBITDA before exit. Scope 2 abatement assumes the user-set grid decarbonization rate is netted from on-site electricity emissions over time. Scope 3 levers (supplier engagement, embodied steel/aluminum) are out of scope for this S1+S2 planner — track separately.
